    Frequently asked questions

    How often should I water raised beds? Depends on weather, but generally 1‑2 inches per week including rainfall. Use a moisture meter.
    What is the best mulch for vegetables? Straw, grass clippings (aged), and leaf mold are excellent. Avoid walnut and eucalyptus.
    When to start seeds indoors? 6‑8 weeks before last frost for tomatoes, peppers; 4‑6 weeks for brassicas.
