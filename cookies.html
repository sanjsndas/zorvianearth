Cookies Policy

We use minimal, respectful cookies to make your visit smooth and personalized. This page explains what we use and why — no hidden surprises.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our content, and improve your experience. We only use cookies that are necessary and respectful of your privacy.

🍪 Cookies we use

Name Purpose Type Duration
session_id Maintains your session and remembers your preferences during your visit. Essential session
pref_theme Remembers your preferred layout or dark/light mode selection. Functionality 1 year
analytics_anon Anonymous usage data to help us improve articles and site structure. Analytics 6 months
newsletter_ref Remembers if you already subscribed to avoid duplicate pop-ups. Functionality 30 days

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or cross-site profiling. Your data stays with us, and we never sell it.

⚙️ Managing your cookies

  • Browser settings: You can block or delete cookies at any time via your browser preferences. Most browsers allow you to control cookie acceptance.
  • Opt-out: For analytics cookies, you can disable them by adjusting your browser settings or using our built-in consent tool (if available).
  • Essential cookies: Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality.

For more information, visit aboutcookies.org or check your browser’s help section.

✅ Your consent

By using our site, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your preferences at any time. We respect your choice.

Third-party cookies

We occasionally embed content from trusted platforms (e.g., YouTube videos, maps). These services may set their own cookies. We do not control these, but we encourage you to review their privacy policies. We only use such embeds when they add real value.

Updates to this policy

We may revise this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in technology or regulations. The latest version will always be available on this page. Last updated: June 30, 2026.
