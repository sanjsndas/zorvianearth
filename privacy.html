Privacy Policy

Your data is safe with us. We believe in transparency, simplicity, and respect for every visitor. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Our commitment to privacy

At Zorvian Earth Living, we are deeply committed to protecting your personal data. We follow strict guidelines and only collect information that helps us improve your experience and deliver the content you love. We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

📌 What we collect

  • Contact information — name, email address, and any details you provide via forms or newsletter signup.
  • Usage data — anonymized information about how you interact with our site (pages visited, time spent, etc.) to help us improve.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — we use minimal cookies to enhance functionality and remember preferences. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

🔐 How we use your data

  • To send you our newsletter (only if you opt-in) and occasional updates about new articles, workshops, or seasonal tips.
  • To respond to your inquiries, comments, or support requests.
  • To analyze site performance and improve our content — all data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or abuse.

🛡️ Data protection & storage

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to safeguard your personal information. Your data is stored only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, after which it is securely deleted.

We do not share your data with third parties except when required by law or to process payments (via trusted, compliant partners).

✅ Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To make a request, simply contact us and we will respond within 30 days.

Children’s privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “last updated” date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
